Visvasa
Brand Identity Guidelines

1. Brand Identity Overview

Visvasahome is a trusted local services brand built on the principles of reliability, transparency, and community trust.

Our identity represents safety in service, respect for skilled professionals, and confidence for customers inviting service providers into their homes and workplaces.

Visvasa exists to make local services organized, accountable, and dependable.

2. Brand Essence

Brand Essence: Trust, Delivered Locally

Brand Positioning:
A technology-driven platform that connects customers with verified local professionals, ensuring quality-controlled services and transparent experiences.

Brand Promise:
Every service booked through Visvasa is backed by verification, clear processes, and human accountability.

3. Core Brand Values
   Trust

Built through verification, consistency, and accountability.

Transparency

Clear pricing, defined scope, honest communication.

Quality

Structured service standards and continuous monitoring.

Local Empowerment

Supporting and strengthening local professionals.

Responsibility

Standing behind every service experience.

4. Brand Personality

Visvasa’s personality should always feel:

Professional

Calm and confident

Dependable

Human-centered

The brand never appears loud, casual, or exaggerated.
It communicates assurance, not urgency.

5. Brand Voice & Tone
   Voice Attributes

Clear and precise

Respectful and reassuring

Informative and composed

Tone Guidelines

Neutral and professional

Helpful without being promotional

Trust-building rather than sales-driven

Language Principles

Use simple, direct sentences

Avoid jargon and slang

Avoid exaggerated claims or hype

6. Logo Usage Guidelines
   Logo Integrity

Use the official Visvasa logo only

Maintain adequate clear space around the logo

Keep proportions intact at all times

Logo Restrictions

Do not stretch, rotate, or distort

Do not change colors or add effects

Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds

7. Color Identity
   Color Philosophy

Visvasa colors represent trust, stability, and professionalism.

Primary colors should feel calm and reliable

Accent colors should support clarity, not distract

Avoid neon or overly playful tones

Consistency across all platforms is mandatory.

8. Typography
   Font Characteristics

Modern, clean, and legible

Optimized for digital readability

Minimal decorative styling

Usage

Headlines: Clear and confident

Body text: Comfortable for long reading

Avoid excessive font variations

9. Imagery & Visual Style
   Image Direction

Authentic people and real services

Real environments over staged setups

Focus on professionalism and care

Avoid

Generic stock imagery

Exaggerated emotions

Unrealistic service scenarios

Visuals should reinforce trust and realism.

10. Iconography & UI Elements

Simple, functional icons

Consistent line style

Purpose-driven design

UI elements should prioritize clarity, ease of use, and accessibility.

11. Brand Applications

These guidelines apply across:

Website and mobile app

Marketing materials

Social media and advertising

Partner and contractor communication

Internal documents

Any deviation weakens brand trust.

12. Brand Governance

Visvasa maintains brand consistency through:

Centralized design standards

Content review processes

Partner and vendor alignment

Unauthorized brand usage is discouraged.

13. Brand Responsibility Statement

Visvasa recognizes that trust is built over time.
Our identity reflects our responsibility to customers, professionals, and communities.

Every touchpoint must reinforce:

Safety

Reliability

Accountability
